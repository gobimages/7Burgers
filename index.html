Our Story

More Than Just Burgers

At 7Burgers, we bring together the best of American fast food and Turkish street food. From juicy burgers and loaded hot dogs to freshly prepared wraps and quality coffee, we're dedicated to serving great food in a relaxed atmosphere. Whether you're dining in, grabbing a takeaway, enjoying a coffee, or meeting friends for a quick bite, 7Burgers is the perfect local spot.

  • 🔥 Grilled fresh to order
  • 📍 Proudly local to New Cross
  • 🌍 American & Turkish flavours
